Work Experiance

Taro Pump (Texmo Industries)

Taro Pump, a prominent division of Texmo Industries, is renowned for manufacturing high-quality pumps and motors. With a focus on innovation, durability, and reliability, Taro Pump serves a wide range of industries, offering products that meet rigorous standards of performance and efficiency. The company is committed to continuous improvement and customer satisfaction, making it a trusted name in the pump manufacturing industry.

At Taro Pump, I operated CNC machines, focusing on precision and accuracy in manufacturing processes such as impeller balancing. My role required meticulous attention to detail to ensure the highest quality of products. Working closely with the production team, we collaborated daily to meet our targets and continuously improve operational efficiency. This teamwork was crucial in maintaining a seamless production process and achieving our goals.

One of the key aspects of my job was implementing and adhering to stringent safety protocols. Safety was a top priority at Taro Pump, and I took pride in contributing to a safe working environment by following best practices and ensuring my colleagues did the same. This focus on safety not only protected us but also ensured that our production processes remained uninterrupted and efficient.

In addition to my technical responsibilities, I also gained valuable experience in problem-solving and troubleshooting. Whenever issues arose with the CNC machines, I worked diligently to diagnose and resolve them, minimizing downtime and maintaining productivity.

Overall, my time at Taro Pump was incredibly rewarding. It allowed me to develop my technical skills, work in a collaborative environment, and contribute to producing high-quality products. The experience taught me the importance of precision, teamwork, and safety in the manufacturing industry.

K.S. Viswanathan & Company

Renowned for its commitment to sustainability, the company utilizes recyclable wooden pulp material to create a diverse range of paper pulp-based products. Their product line includes plates, cups, toys, dolls, packaging products, and various craft items, all designed with an eco-friendly approach. By focusing on sustainable materials, KS Viswanathan & Company not only contributes to environmental conservation but also sets a benchmark for innovation in the industry. Their dedication to producing high-quality, sustainable products ensures they meet the growing demand for environmentally responsible solutions while maintaining excellence in design and functionality.
